About me

I am Yuzhou Tong, a MSc student at Nanyang Technological University (NTU). I am currently studying in the programme of Communications Engineering in the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering.

I obtained my Bachelor's Degree at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-SZ) in June 2022. From June 2020 to June 2022, I worked as a research assistant (RA) at the Network Coding Lab at CUHK-Shenzhen (supervisor: Prof. Shenghao Yang). My work there was mainly about research and development of Wi-Fi systems.

From Feb 2023 to Jun 2023, I worked as an RA in the Singapore-ETH Center, Future Resilient Systems supervised by Prof.Eleni Chatzi. I was mainly in charge of the development of the digital mobile sensing platform (DMSP). A demo video could be found here.

From Aug 2023 to the present, I have been working as an RA while doing my Master's dissertation under the supervision of Prof. Zhiping Lin. The topic of my dissertation is "Urban Anomaly Detection and Human Activity Classification with Cellular Base-station Traffic".

I am seeking currently for a PhD position related to machine learning/wireless communications/generative models in the fall 2024. My brief CV is available here.
